Meet your instructor

Welcome to my macrame classes! I'm Ronja, based in Berlin, and I'm excited to share my passion for this beautiful craft with you. My journey with macrame began in the most unexpected way—learning from a group of Argentinian nomads while traveling through the stunning landscapes of Bolivia. This enchanting art form captivated me instantly, and I continued my exploration through various tutorials and personal projects. Macrame has become more than just a craft for me; it's a way to clear my mind, relax, and create unique jewelry pieces that reflect my individual style. I am thrilled to offer beginner courses in a region where this technique is not widely known, creating wonderful moments and beautiful macrame pieces together!
